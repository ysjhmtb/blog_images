## Temperature Effects


$$
R_{2}=\dfrac {T_{2}-T_{0}}{T_{1}-T_{0}}R_{1}
$$

$$
R_{2}=R_{1}\left[ 1+\alpha _{1}\left( T_{2}-T_{1}\right) \right]
$$
